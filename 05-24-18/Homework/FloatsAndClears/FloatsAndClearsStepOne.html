Look at the divs below. Currently they don't have any float attribute. When you click the button they'll add float:left to the L columns and float: right to the right columns. Before you click the button try to figure out where they'll go and draw it on a piece of paper if needed to check if you're right.

Did they do what you thought they would? If so, awesome! If not, no worries. Remember that floating elements will try to fill either the available space on the "line" directly above itself OR start a new "line" if there isn't enough room.
